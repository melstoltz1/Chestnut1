Twosie Tuesday

Posted on February 2, 2016 by admin

I am proud to introduce “Twosie Tuesday”!

This is the tacky name for an opportunity for serious savings when it comes to fish buying.  You can pick out one fish and get a second fish of equal or lesser value free.  This offer does not extend to the feeder fish who are already so low I would nearly have to pay you to take them to drop the price farther.  OK, that is an exaggeration but 5 feeder fish for a dollar is very low you must admit.  To get back to the regular thrust of this post, you can come in on any Tuesday and the fish will be waiting for you to take them home for one half the normal cost!
